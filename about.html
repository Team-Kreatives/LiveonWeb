About Us

Our little story

LiveOn represents a group of creative minds with a driven passion to provide, guide and relay meaningful experiences to individuals concerning the allocation of various assets/properties and the implementations of Wills to suit their decisions. We understand that death can be inevitable and unprecedented and as such, people deserve this opportunity to make impactful decisions regarding their assets/properties with the purpose of achieving a suitable Will. This in turn will promote transparency amongst their families and reduce disputes.
